What is Reciplan

ReciPlan is an innovative, open-source Salesforce application designed to empower individuals with AI-driven recipe generation and comprehensive meal management capabilities. Leveraging the robust Salesforce platform, ReciPlan integrates advanced AI functionalities (likely via external services or Apex-driven integrations) to provide personalized recipe suggestions based on available ingredients, dietary preferences, and real-time food inventory.

The project aims to demonstrate how Salesforce can be extended beyond traditional CRM to solve everyday problems, foster healthier eating habits, reduce food waste, and streamline culinary processes for a variety of users. Being open-source, ReciPlan encourages community contributions, allowing developers to extend its functionality, integrate with new AI models, or tailor it to specific industry needs.

ReciPlan: Your AI-Powered Personal Culinary Assistant

ReciPlan is an innovative and intelligent mobile and web application designed to transform the way individuals approach cooking and meal preparation. At its heart, ReciPlan leverages cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence to provide personalized recipe generation, making healthy, delicious, and resource-efficient cooking accessible to everyone. The project aims to simplify meal planning, reduce food waste by utilizing available ingredients, and inspire culinary creativity through intuitive and interactive features.

Core AI-Powered Recipe Generation

The central feature of ReciPlan is its sophisticated AI recipe generation engine. This engine takes various user inputs and crafts unique, tailored recipes:

  • Intelligent Ingredient-Based Recipes: Users simply input the ingredients they currently have on hand. The AI processes this list, cross-referencing it with a vast database of culinary knowledge to propose recipes that maximally utilize the provided items. This is a core mechanism for reducing food waste.
  • Personalized Dietary & Preference Customization: The AI adapts its recipe suggestions based on a comprehensive range of user-defined dietary restrictions (e.g., Vegetarian, Vegan, Gluten-Free, Keto, Dairy-Free, Nut-Free) and meal preferences (e.g., cuisine type, cooking style, quick meals). This ensures that every generated recipe aligns perfectly with the user's health goals and tastes.
  • Dynamic Meal Type & Serving Size Adjustments: Users can specify the desired meal type (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snack, Dessert) and the number of servings needed. The AI adjusts the ingredient quantities and recipe complexity accordingly, making it suitable for individuals, couples, or families.

Intuitive Multi-Modal Ingredient Input

ReciPlan offers revolutionary ways to input ingredients, moving beyond traditional manual entry for ultimate convenience:

  • Voice Recognition: Users can simply speak the names of the ingredients they have. The app's integrated voice recognition (Speech-to-Text) technology accurately transcribes the spoken words, converting them into a structured list that the AI can understand. This hands-free input is perfect for when you're in the kitchen.
  • Image Upload & Recognition: A groundbreaking feature allows users to take a picture of their available ingredients. The app utilizes advanced Image Recognition AI (e.g., computer vision models) to identify and list the food items detected in the photograph. This streamlines the inventory process, making it incredibly fast and efficient.

Comprehensive Recipe Management & Discovery

Beyond generation, ReciPlan empowers users to explore, manage, and engage with culinary content:

  • Extensive Ingredient Search: Users can search for specific ingredients (e.g., "chicken," "broccoli," "lentils") to discover a wide array of recipes that feature them. This is ideal for when you have a core ingredient and are looking for inspiration.
  • Detailed Step-by-Step Instructions: Every generated or discovered recipe comes with clear, concise, and easy-to-follow preparation steps, ensuring even novice cooks can achieve great results.

Immersive Cooking Experience & Assistance

ReciPlan transforms the cooking process itself with interactive and assistive features:

  • Interactive Voice Assistant for Cooking: A dedicated voice assistant can read aloud the recipe steps, allowing users to cook hands-free without needing to constantly look at their screen. This is invaluable for maintaining hygiene and focus during cooking.
  • Voice Commands & Controls: Users have full control over the voice assistant's playback, with intuitive voice commands like "Start," "Stop," "Next Step," "Previous Step," and "Repeat" alongside on-screen buttons, providing a truly interactive and convenient cooking guide.

Integrated Multimedia & Learning Hub

ReciPlan extends its utility by integrating with popular multimedia platforms, fostering a rich learning environment:

  • YouTube Integration for Culinary Content: Users can create and manage personalized playlists of their favorite cooking videos directly within the app, centralizing their go-to visual guides.
  • YouTube Channel Subscriptions: The app allows users to subscribe to cooking-related YouTube channels, providing a curated feed of new content and allowing them to follow their favorite chefs and food bloggers without leaving ReciPlan.

What's Cooking Next: ReciPlan's Upcoming Features - A Detailed Explanation

We are thrilled to give you an in-depth look at the innovative features slated for ReciPlan's next major release. These enhancements are designed to make your culinary journey even more connected, health-conscious, and convenient, truly embodying our vision of a comprehensive personal cooking assistant.

1. WhatsApp Notification Integration

Functionality:

This feature will seamlessly connect ReciPlan's core functionalities with your WhatsApp account, leveraging the ubiquitous messaging platform for real-time, actionable alerts. Users will have the option to opt-in for various types of notifications, ensuring they receive only the information most relevant to them. The integration will utilize secure API endpoints to send messages directly from ReciPlan to your designated WhatsApp number.

Benefits:

  • Enhanced Convenience: Receive critical updates without needing to open the ReciPlan app, making information readily accessible during busy moments or while away from the screen.
  • Improved Meal Planning Adherence: Timely reminders can significantly boost consistency in following meal plans and preparing meals on schedule.
  • Reduced Food Waste (Potential): Alerts for expiring ingredients (if integrated with an advanced inventory feature) or upcoming meal prep can help users utilize food before it spoils.
  • Streamlined Collaboration: Easily share auto-generated shopping lists or even specific recipes with family members or housemates who are also on WhatsApp, simplifying coordinated grocery runs or meal prep.
  • Interactive Cooking Assistance: Imagine receiving step-by-step cooking instructions via WhatsApp, allowing for a truly hands-free cooking experience when you don't want to touch your device.

User Experience:

Users would typically enable this feature within ReciPlan's settings, linking their WhatsApp number. They could then customize notification preferences, such as opting to receive reminders 30 minutes before a planned dinner, alerts when a saved recipe's key ingredient is low, or quick links to shopping lists shared by other household members.

2. Integrated Health Tracker

Functionality:

The Health Tracker aims to transform ReciPlan from just a recipe generator into a comprehensive dietary wellness companion. It will integrate nutritional data directly into the recipe generation and meal planning process. This involves:

  • Nutritional Database: A robust backend database containing detailed nutritional information for a vast array of ingredients and common dishes.
  • AI-Driven Analysis: When a recipe is generated or selected, the AI will calculate its nutritional profile (calories, macronutrients, key vitamins/minerals) based on ingredients and portion sizes.
  • Goal Setting & Monitoring: Users will be able to set personal dietary goals (e.g., daily calorie targets, protein intake, limits on sugar/fat, specific allergen avoidance). The tracker will then monitor their intake against these goals for meals logged through ReciPlan.
  • Historical Data & Trends: The system will store logged meal data, allowing users to view their nutritional history, identify patterns, and track progress over time through intuitive charts and summaries.

Benefits:

  • Informed Food Choices: Empower users to understand the nutritional impact of their meals, leading to healthier eating habits.
  • Personalized Wellness: Tailor recipe suggestions not just by ingredients and preferences, but also by health goals, directly contributing to weight management, athletic performance, or managing specific health conditions.
  • Allergen Safety: Critical for users with allergies or sensitivities, providing immediate alerts for potentially harmful ingredients in recipes.
  • Motivation & Accountability: Visual progress reports can serve as a powerful motivator, helping users stay accountable to their dietary objectives.
  • Reduced Reliance on Multiple Apps: Consolidate recipe management and basic health tracking into one platform.

User Experience:

Within their ReciPlan profile, users would input their health goals, dietary restrictions, and allergies. When generating a recipe, they would see an immediate nutritional summary. Post-meal, they could quickly log their consumption, and the Health Tracker would update their daily/weekly progress, showing visual dashboards of their intake relative to their targets.

3. Zomato Ordering Integration

Functionality:

This feature acknowledges that even the most dedicated home cooks sometimes need a break or require ingredients not on hand. The Zomato integration will provide a seamless bridge between ReciPlan's meal planning and the convenience of food delivery.

  • Contextual Ordering Links: If a user is viewing a recipe that requires a specific ingredient they lack, or if they decide at the last minute not to cook, ReciPlan could offer a direct link to Zomato to order that ingredient (if available for delivery) or to find restaurants offering similar dishes.
  • Preference-Based Discovery: Leveraging a user's ReciPlan dietary preferences (e.g., vegetarian, gluten-free, specific cuisines), the integration can intelligently suggest relevant Zomato restaurants or dishes that align with their established tastes.
  • Quick Switch Mechanism: A prominent "Order from Zomato" button or similar call-to-action would be available within certain contexts, allowing for a rapid transition to the Zomato app or website with pre-filled search queries.

Benefits:

  • Ultimate Flexibility: Provides users with a convenient alternative when cooking isn't feasible or desired, without leaving the ReciPlan ecosystem.
  • Time-Saving: Reduces the friction of switching between apps and manually searching for relevant food options.
  • Enhanced Convenience: Acts as a helpful "Plan B" for mealtime, ensuring users can always find a solution for their hunger, whether it's cooking or ordering.
  • Personalized Recommendations: Leverage ReciPlan's understanding of user preferences to suggest takeout options that genuinely appeal to them.

User Experience:

A user browsing a recipe for, say, "Chicken Curry" might see an option pop up: "Don't have chicken? Order chicken curry from Zomato!" Or, when creating a meal plan, they could easily mark a day as "Order Out" and ReciPlan could suggest Zomato options based on their favorite cuisines or past ordered dishes. The transition would be smooth, either opening the Zomato app directly to a search result or redirecting to the Zomato website.

ReciPlan Architecture: A Holistic View

ReciPlan is designed as a robust, scalable, and intelligent application built primarily on the Salesforce Platform, extended through external AI services and various third-party integrations. This hybrid architecture allows ReciPlan to leverage Salesforce's powerful CRM capabilities for data management and user interface while tapping into specialized AI models for complex tasks like recipe generation and multimedia recognition.

1. Salesforce Platform (Core Layer Or System Landscape)

The Salesforce Platform forms the foundational core of ReciPlan, handling data storage, business logic, user interface, and security.

  • Lightning Web Components (LWC): The primary technology for building the modern, responsive, and interactive user interface (UI) of ReciPlan. LWCs handle user interactions, display data, and send inputs.
  • Apex (Backend Logic): Salesforce's proprietary programming language acting as the orchestrator. Apex handles data manipulation, secure communication with external APIs, and core business logic.
  • Salesforce Objects (Data Model): Custom and standard objects used to structure and store all application-specific data, including user preferences, ingredients, recipes, and meal plans.
  • Salesforce APIs: Enable programmatic access to Salesforce data and metadata, facilitating integrations and custom development.

2. AI & Machine Learning Services (External Layer)

ReciPlan leverages specialized external AI services to perform complex intelligent tasks, maintaining a lean Salesforce footprint for heavy computation.

  • Recipe Generation AI: A large language model (LLM) that takes user inputs and generates novel, coherent, and practical recipes.
  • Image Recognition AI: Computer Vision models that process images uploaded by users to identify and list edible items.
  • Speech-to-Text (Voice Recognition) AI: Services that transcribe spoken ingredient lists or cooking commands into text.
  • Nutritional Analysis AI/Database: Calculates comprehensive nutritional data for generated or user-inputted recipes.

3. Third-Party Integrations

ReciPlan connects with external services to provide an enriched and seamless user experience.

  • YouTube API: Integrates culinary video content for learning and inspiration.

4. Data Flow Overview

User input from the LWC UI is sent to an Apex controller. Apex then orchestrates secure API calls to external AI services (for recipe generation, image/voice recognition) or third-party platforms (YOUTUBE). The processed data is returned to Apex, which updates Salesforce Objects for storage, and finally, sends the relevant information back to the LWC for display. Notifications are triggered by Apex calls to the WhatsApp API.

Setup

These are the steps to get ReciPlan up and running in your Salesforce org.

  1. Set up your environment. Follow the steps in the Quick Start: Lightning Web Components Trailhead project. The steps include:

    • Enable Dev Hub in your Trailhead Playground
    • Install Salesforce CLI
    • Install Visual Studio Code
    • Install the Visual Studio Code Salesforce extensions, including the Lightning Web Components extension.

  2. If you haven't already done so, authenticate with your hub org and provide it with an alias (myhuborg) in the command below:

    sfdx force:auth:web:login -d -a myhuborg

  3. Clone the ReciPlan repository:

    git clone https://github.com/YourRepo/ReciPlan.git
cd ReciPlan

  4. Create a scratch org and provide it with an alias (reciplanorg in the command below):

    sfdx force:org:create -f config/project-scratch-def.json -a reciplanorg

  5. Push the app to your scratch org:

    sfdx force:source:push
